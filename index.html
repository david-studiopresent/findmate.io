Technical Specifications

Mobile Apps: FindMate works with iOS 12+ and Android 8.0+. The app is optimized for both smartphones and tablets.

Hardware Connection: Connects via Bluetooth 5.0 for maximum compatibility and energy efficiency. Range up to 10 meters between your phone and the FindMate cube.

iOS: 12.0+ (iPhone 5s or newer)
Android: 8.0+ (API level 26+)
Bluetooth: 5.0 LE required

Technology: Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) RFID tags operating at 915 MHz (US) / 868 MHz (EU) for superior range and reliability.

No Batteries Needed: Tags are completely passive - powered by the electromagnetic field from the FindMate cube's antenna when scanning.

Reading Range: Up to 3 meters (10 feet)
Tag Size: 25mm × 15mm × 0.8mm
Adhesive: Industrial 3M adhesive backing
Durability: Water-resistant, -20°C to +70°C

Battery Performance: The compact 13×13cm cube houses a high-capacity lithium polymer battery optimized for RFID scanning operations.

Battery Life: Up to 2 weeks (typical use)
Charging Time: Under 2 hours (0-100%)
Charging Port: USB-C (cable included)
Standby Time: Up to 3 months

Power Management: Intelligent sleep mode activates after 5 minutes of inactivity. LED indicator shows battery status and charging progress.

Usage & Support

Unlimited Tracking: Track as many items as you want - no artificial limits on the number of tags or sets you can create.

1

Download & Pair

Download the app, create account, and pair your FindMate cube via Bluetooth.

2

Tag Your Items

Attach RFID tags to items and scan them to register in the app.

3

Create Sets

Group items into sets like "Camera Kit", "Travel Essentials", etc.

4

Scan & Verify

Place FindMate cube near your bag and scan to verify all items are present.

Setup Time: Complete setup typically takes 10-15 minutes. Each new item takes 30 seconds to tag and register.

Fully Offline Operation: FindMate works completely offline - no internet connection required for scanning or verification.

🔒

Local Data Storage

All your item lists and sets are stored locally on your device. No cloud dependency.

🛡️

Zero Tracking
